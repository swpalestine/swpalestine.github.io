Our Purpose

We are a collective of passionate activists, community organizers, and everyday citizens united by a common goal: to amplify Palestinian voices and promote lasting peace through education, awareness, and humanitarian support.

Who We Are

We are an independent, non-party political organisation, run by volunteers all committed to a just and sustainable peace in the Middle East.

Solidarity Palestine is a community-driven initiative dedicated to raising awareness, fostering understanding, and supporting the rights and dignity of the Palestinian people. Rooted in the principles of justice, equality, and human rights, we aim to amplify the voices of those striving for peace and freedom in Palestine.

We believe in the power of collective action and the importance of a just world where all people can live in peace and security. Together, we strive to create meaningful change and uphold the values of solidarity, compassion, and resilience.

What We Do

  • Campaigning with activities locally and worldwide by prepairing and disseminating Solidarity Palestine statements and public speaking
  • Media Outreach in Palestine and abroad, based on a professional media strategy
  • Lobbying the media
  • Coordination with SP activists locally and worldwide, including preparation of regional and international organizing meetings and conferences
  • Organising boycott actions and pickets
  • Organising public lectures, debates, discussion evenings, workshops and media appearances
